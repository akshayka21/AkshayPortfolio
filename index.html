Projects

SayCheezz-DSLR Camera Rentals Website and Mobile App

SayCheezz was a crazy idea from a few college graduates, where they wanted to give cameras for rent. In today's growing competition every 3 to 6 months, there will be a new camera in the market. Users don't like to buy a camera, even if they buy, they may use only when they go for trips and a few occasions after which it will be in some corner of the house. Customers who just want to rent a camera can visit the website, select the dates they need, check whether its available on such dates and if available they will be able to book the camera. Delivery boys will deliver it to their home and pick it up back after the usage. All the customer documents will be uploaded and collected on the web portal itself for safety and theft.


WWW: https://www.saycheezz.in/
Mob: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aitron.tech.saycheezz
Role: Android Developer.
Platform: Kotlin, Android Studio.
